Heliosphere - The Secret of The Maze
BETA release in fall 2023

Development
The development of Heliosphere-The Secret of The Maze began on May 2023, inaugurating the start of SLHIDE™. The game's title was chosen after the space-like theme of the game and the hidden content inside the Labyrinth. Although Heliosphere is planned to get an Android version, the game will be first released on PC version (Windows).


Gameplay
  Heliosphere - The Secret of The Maze is a multiplayer Hide&Seek game, in which 2 teams have to find the other one in turn in a wide maze, full of unique powerups and secrets...
 At the beginning of the game, a random team attacks the other one during a certain amount of time and then switch the roles until noone is remaining, while the other one must hide and level up their character.
By levelling up, the player unlocks characters that have better starting stats. Of course, each player can upgrade his character during the game and try to max him out to get a better attack, a better defense and move faster.
 In order to start a game,someone must host a lobby and wait for other people to join it. While waiting for the game to start, players can pick their team. The game can be played with up to 10 players, with balanced teams. If the lobby is too empty, the host can choose to fill it with bots.


Powerups
Dashpad : Increases the player's speed over a certain distance and time
Invisibility : Turns the player invisible over a limited time
Regeneration : Offers some HP to heal the player
Vision : Increases the player's vision range over a limited time
Phantom : Gives the ability to the player to pass through over a limited time
Radar : Allows the player to see his enemies on the map over a limited time
Teleportation : Makes the player teleport from one point to another one
Resurrection : Allows the player to resurrect one of his team mates when he passes by his mark
