Gameplay
 Helioshere - The Secret of The Maze is a multiplayer Hide N Seek game, where 2 teams will have to find the other one in turn in a maze. The maze features cool powers that the player can use whenever he wants.

 At the beginning of the game, there is a grace period, and after that, a random team attacks the other one during a random amount of time, while the other one must hide. In the meantime, each player can collect items, earn EXP, have fun,.. To avoid camping, little ennemies (only visible on the map) will chase the nearest player and make him loose HP.


 The player can unlock characters that have better starting stats by completing achievements and unique characters with secret achievements.

 Of course, each player can upgrade his character during the game and try to max him.

 Someone hosts a lobby and the others join it. While waiting, the players can choose which team they will belong too. The lobbies will accept 10 players maximum (5 vs 5), and the host will have the ability to check "fill with bots" in order to make the game playable, even alone or at 2.





Items / Powerups
Dashpads : Dashpads increase the player speed on a limited range and change the player's direction to his own.

Invisibility : Once activated, this item allows you to turn invisible to others during a limited time.

Shield : Once activated, this item offers you a shield that can be broken in the following seconds.

Vision Increased : This item, once activated, offers you a wided vision during a limited time.

Phantom : Once activated, this item allows you to go through walls for a limited time.

Teleportation : Each teleportation spot teleports you to linked one.

Radar : It allows you to see the nearby players on the map even if they are invisible.

Resurrection : You can resurrect a player of your team by activating it but both of you will have their stats drown back to their initial.

Player Stats
Attack : The more it is upgraded, the more damage you heal to enemies, and the bigger is your damage zone.

Defense : The more it is upgraded, the bigger is your HP amount, and the lesser players make damage to you.

Speed : The more it is upgraded, the faster you go and the more powerful dashpads become.
